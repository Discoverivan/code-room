# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus feedback on the work.
- Respect privacy and never publish room links or document contents.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and correct mistakes.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and publishing private
information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository owner's GitHub
profile. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed.
